# _

The idea

Backdoor is for people who want to understand systems instead of worshipping tools. It is about thinking clearly, breaking carefully, and learning from reality rather than myths.

What happens here

The project brings together people interested in penetration testing, programming, and infrastructure. Beginners learn how things actually work. Experienced members sharpen their thinking by explaining, debating, and testing assumptions.

Pentesting mindset

Methodology, threat modeling, and reasoning before exploitation.

Engineering depth

Operating systems, networks, and code beyond surface-level tutorials.

Ethics and responsibility

Understanding where curiosity ends and responsibility begins.

Open access

Backdoor is open to those who respect learning and ethics. No ranks, no posturing — just people trying to understand complex systems.
